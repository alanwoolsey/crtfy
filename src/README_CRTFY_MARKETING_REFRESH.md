# Crtfy Website Marketing Refresh

This source update repositions the site around the new Crtfy platform story:

- **Crtfy Student**: admissions operating system / CRM replacement story
- **Crtfy AI**: governed AI workspace for institutional teams
- **Crtfy Content**: intelligent content management for education

Key changes:

1. Rewrote the homepage hero and product narrative.
2. Replaced the old “three SaaS applications” story with a connected platform story.
3. Updated Crtfy Documents positioning to **Crtfy Content** while keeping the existing `/products/crtfy-documents` route so current links do not break.
4. Rebuilt the products page around the adoption path: admissions CRM replacement, governed AI adoption, and ECM modernization.
5. Rebuilt the Crtfy Student page around Today’s Work, Student 360, Transcript Intelligence, Decision Studio, Trust Center, and Yield/Handoff.
6. Rebuilt generic product pages for Crtfy AI and Crtfy Content with product-specific lifecycle sections.
7. Added marketing-focused CSS classes for the new platform preview, journey strips, comparison cards, and refreshed product cards.
8. Avoided customer logo claims and instead uses experience/platform positioning until logo permissions are secured.
